9 Orbis' Gospel: Supernova. Episode III

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

After the battle against Jack Brave, Orbis returns to its years of peace. Adam is now in charge of enforcing the new Laws of Coexistence between dimensions due to the pact with Lux. Novum has been dissolved due to the era of peace that now resides. The demons have decided to be peaceful entities. However, there are those who still oppose this.

Avernis Blakthus, in charge of a city raised in the southern continent Malitae, Lunae Lumen, finds himself in the need to face a new unknown enemy who attacks the city.

This subject claims that all those related to demons must succumb, which is a violation of the Laws of Coexistence between dimensions. This battle will mark a new era in Orbis and in all dimensions.

Years in which the project takes place:

Prologue:
2074/03/15. A strange subject appears who attacks people who maintain a relationship with demons. Avernis Blakthus decides to confront him.
Story:
2074/03/15. The subject turned out to be David, Adam's clone. The latter appears just when Avernis ends David's life. However, Adam decides to confront Avernis to avenge David's death. A battle between the human who reached a god level and the god who lost his humanity.
Epilogue:
0000/01/01. In the different turns of events and the confrontation against Cosmos. Adam makes a pact that mankind can no longer make use of spiritual energy and that the dimensions can no longer communicate with each other. Avernis returns to Orbis, a world that is reborn again without demons or angels. A world where humans will carry out his will.
