5 Orbis' Gospel: Red Giant

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

Suddenly, an apparent soldier and his group take control of the Orbis Government. Cain Strongwilled, who is leading this group, comments that he is another clone created from the results obtained from David.

Cain, who decides to continue with his father's project, looks for people who can use spiritual energy in the different cities of Orbis to start the NeoGenesis project. David, who hears of this, goes to confront him, however, he loses his right arm in combat and retreats to Novum.

In Novum, Christina tells David the details behind the NeoGenesis project. David, who now has a prosthetic arm, decides to stop the actions Cain is taking as the new President of the Orbis Government.

Years in which the project takes place:

Prologue:
2057/03/12. Cain heads to the Royal Palace in Civitas to take command of the Orbis Government.
Story:
2057/05/16 - 2057/09/25. The development of the story, which includes the first confrontation between David and Cain, the meeting with Christina and David's training in Novum and how David defeats the platoons that are sent to the different cities of Orbis to be subdued.
Epilogue:
2055/09/25. A Jack Brave is shown watching the final battle between clones who, in turn, recalls how all the events that have occurred over the past few years are turning out just as he had planned all his life.
