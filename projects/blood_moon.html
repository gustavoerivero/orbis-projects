6 Orbis' Gospel: Blood Moon

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

In the end of the famous Pillar Wars, those that are considered a before and after in Orbis, there was one of the last kings, Vlad Mokei. However, the sacred army would put an end to Vlad's peace by destroying his castle and with it, his family.

Vlad, in an impulse of anger and desperation, makes a demonic pact, thus beginning the peak of evil at that time in Orbis. Despite this, Vlad, realizing that he cannot bring back all that he lost, regrets the decisions he made.

Years later, Vlad who now calls himself Jack Brave, who has succumbed completely to hatred and anger towards humans, sets out on a journey in order to completely destroy those who took everything from him.
