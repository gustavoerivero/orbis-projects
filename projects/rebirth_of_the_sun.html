4 Orbis' Gospel: Rebirth of the Sun

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

Adam and a huge squadron of angels are sent to Orbis with the mission to destroy the clone that the Orbis Government is creating before it is born. However, the clone is born and Adam decides to withdraw from the battle.

Sixteen years later, David Meddler, a young man adopted by President Michael Strongwilled, discovers that he has strange abilities to manipulate spiritual energy. The President, who learns of David's abilities from David's comments, sends a special squad to capture David for use in experiments. Witnessing his adoptive father turn his back on him, David escapes.

David meets with a group of rebels who oppose the reality imposed by the Orbis Government and there, he realizes that there are many things in the world that he has yet to discover, such as his own identity and origin.

Years in which the project takes place:

Prologue:
2039/12/31. Adam and the angels are sent to destroy the clone of the Orbis Government.
Story:
2055/06/05 - 2055/10/12. The development of the story that includes the discovery of David and his powers, the struggle of the rebel group, the death of Anastasia and the final battle between David and the President.
Epilogue:
2055/11/21. David encounters Adam and they have a strong conversation. Meanwhile, eyes that emanate an energy similar to David's swear revenge.
