1 Orbis' Gospel: Paradise Downfall

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

After 25 years of a great and arduous battle between a mysterious subject and a very powerful demon, two individuals were born; Eve, as a demon summoned with a woman's body and great abilities, and Adam, a human with an ability to adapt to situations faster than usual.

Eve is on a quest to gather the pieces so she can summon the Monarch of Hell, Lucifer, who is desperate to get his revenge after his exile from Paradise in the Battle of a Thousand Years and take control of Paradise.

Adam, meanwhile, had no idea what was going on around him until he witnessed events in a progressive way that prompted him to get involved in this battle of dimensional entities and stop Eve on her way to bring Lucifer to Orbis.

Years in which the project takes place:

Prologue:
2016/12/12. With the birth of Adam and Eve.
Story:
2035/02/03 - 2039/10/29. The development of the story, including Adam's coma and his return, as well as the final battle against Lucifer in Paradise.
Epilogue:
2039/10/29. Eve finds herself in Paradise and frees Lucifer from his prison without anyone noticing.
