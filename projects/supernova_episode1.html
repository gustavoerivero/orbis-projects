7 Orbis' Gospel: Supernova. Episode I

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

Peace returns to Orbis after years of wars and battles. However, this peace is interrupted after the awakening of Chaos, a god of the underworld who has been in a deep sleep for thousands of years.

Chaos was divided into three entities in the past and now he will seek to reunite the pieces of the relic that will allow the fusion of these entities and thus become whole again.

Ventus Inferos, Malum Petram and Juravit Malum, the divided entities of Chaos, where each one will go to a specific dimension to recover the pieces of the relic that sealed and divided it.

On the other hand, Paradise, Hell and Orbis feel the awakening of the parts of Chaos, so they prepare for one of the most difficult confrontations of their lives.

Years in which the project takes place:

Prologue:
2060/06/02. A small glimpse of the current status of the characters. The awakening of the entities that conform Chaos and how the evil energy of this god affects the characters.
Story:
2060/06/02 - 2060/06/12. Character battles against Chaos entities in their respective dimensions. The final battle against Chaos in Orbis, where Adam makes a pact with Cosmos in order to face Chaos in an equally way.
Epilogue:
2060/06/12. Jack Brave acquires new abilities and powers as the man in charge of the Infernal Realm.
