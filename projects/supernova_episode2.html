8 Orbis' Gospel: Supernova. Episode II

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

After an arduous battle, where Adam unleashed abilities from a pact with Cosmos in order to stop Chaos once and for all, a new evil is unleashed in Orbis. Only a few kilometers away from the battlefield against Chaos, Jack Brave assumes the Reign of Hell due to the Infernal Laws.

Jack invokes Yggdrasil, the sacred tree that provides the Forbidden Fruit, a fruit that grants ascension to godhood to the individual who eats it.

All those who battled against Chaos, especially Adam, have not recovered from their wounds and the energies spent in their recent fight, however, they will have to gather strength to fight once again against the infernal forces of Jack and his Horsemen of the Apocalypse.
