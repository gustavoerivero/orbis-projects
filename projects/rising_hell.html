2 Orbis' Gospel: Rising Hell

@gustavoerivero

Synopsis:

Adam finds himself in Orbis telling the Novum members who survived the battle that Lucifer has been sealed and that he died as a human and was "reborn" as a Seraphim. However, Adam senses that something bad is happening in Paradise and returns to see what is going on.

Upon returning to Paradise, he learns that Lucifer has been released and performed a pocession seal in the dimension. Adam goes directly to fight Lucifer, however, Lucifer has obtained his former Cherubic abilities and sends all the angels and Adam to Hell.

Adam wakes up in Hell and realizes that he can neither use his holy abilities nor summon his weapon, Sanctus. So Adam will be in Hell on a quest to ascend to Orbis and return to Paradise to defeat Lucifer once and for all.

Years in which the project takes place:

Prologue:
2039/10/29. Adam is sent to Hell.
Story:
2039/10/29 - 2039/12/31. The development of the story, including Adam's stay in Hell, his return to Orbis, ascent to Paradise and final battle against Lucifer.
Epilogue:
2039/12/31. Conversation between Adam and Jack about the current situation of Orbis and how Jack's past influenced everything that happened.
